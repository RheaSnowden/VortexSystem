# How VORTEX Works

VORTEX is a **thermosiphon-driven, dual-phase tunnel system** that harvests energy from both falling water and rising steam in one integrated loop. It turns waste heat into electricity while recycling water and managing thermal energy at scale.

### The Basic Cycle (Simple Version)
1. **Heat Input** — Waste heat (industrial, nuclear, geothermal, etc.) is added at the bottom or along the tunnel.
2. **Evaporation** — Water is heated and turns into steam, creating upward flow.
3. **Steam Harvesting** — Rising steam passes through modular **steam sleeves** that extract kinetic energy and generate electricity.
4. **Condensation** — At the top (or in cooler upper sections), steam condenses back into water, often aided by external radiators or heat exchangers.
5. **Water Descent** — Liquid water flows down the sloped canals through **hydro sleeves**, generating more electricity via gravity and flow.
6. **Return & Reuse** — The cycle repeats. Excess heat is cascaded into useful thermal loops (heating, industry, agriculture).

The result is a continuous, self-sustaining loop that produces power while recycling water and capturing heat that would otherwise be wasted.

### Key Engineering Features
- **Inclined Tunnel** (30–45°): Provides reliable gravity drive. Can be straight, oval loops, or stacked spirals.
- **Hydro Sleeves** (lower section): Extract energy from downward liquid flow. Gradient spiral fins, modular, quick-swap.
- **Steam Sleeves** (upper section): Harvest energy from rising vapor. Mounted on center supports.
- **Stepped Basins & Heat Loops**: Control flow, enable additional evaporation, and distribute heat at different temperature grades.
- **Maintenance Access**: Central rail + crane system allows sleeve replacement in minutes without full shutdown.
- **Mass & Heat Balance**: Automated valves, overflow returns, and optional external water/steam injection keep the system stable.

### Why It’s Effective
- **Distributed** — Many small sleeves instead of one large turbine = high availability and easy maintenance.
- **Closed-Loop** — Minimal external water needs once primed.
- **Multi-Use** — Produces both electricity and usable heat.
- **Scalable** — Works from single tunnels to large arcology or industrial park networks.
