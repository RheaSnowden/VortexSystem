# VORTEX System
(Vertical Open Rotation Tunnel for Energy eXtraction)

**Modular inclined tunnel system for waste heat recovery, distributed power generation, and closed-loop thermal management.**

VORTEX turns industrial and residential waste heat into electricity while enabling highly efficient, circular resource flows. Designed for integration with arcologies, industrial energy parks, and future space habitats.

[View Full Summary →](docs/vortex-core/summary.md)

## Key Features
- Distributed sleeve turbines for high availability and easy maintenance
- Closed-loop water and heat recycling
- Scalable from single tunnels to networked arcology spines
- Strong synergy with nuclear, industrial waste heat, and carbon capture
- Adaptable to Earth and off-world applications

## Core Concepts
- Thermosiphon + gravity-driven flow
- Hydro and steam sleeve energy harvesting
- Cascading multi-grade heat loops
- Modular, quick-swap components with rail maintenance access
